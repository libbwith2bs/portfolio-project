Hi, I'm Elizabeth Tutewiler

Executive Assistant, based in Normal, IL

You're probably wondering why there are so many projects on this website about an Executive Assitant. Well, after years in communications and software management, I put my career on hold to stay home with my children. As the time for them to begin school neared, I decided to expand my communications knowledge by learning how to code. Through SheCodes, I learned the basics of front end work in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and the React library. When looking for careers in the web development field, I got my foot in the door at a local web design company as an Executive Assistant to the CEO. While not the job I initially planned for my career path, I swiftly grew to love the role. Providing support to help growth happen more quickly, learning new skills in various fields to become a jack of all trades, and being the person everyone can come to when a solution is needed has been such a rewarding experience. So yes, this is a portfolio of a skillset I possess that has nothing to do with my current career, but it's a testament to the fact that I'm eager to learn and capable of pivoting well.
